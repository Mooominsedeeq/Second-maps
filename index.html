Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym timings?

We are open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. Sunday timings are 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

Is the gym safe and friendly for women?

Absolutely. We pride ourselves on maintaining a strictly professional, respectful, and highly secure environment. We have dedicated changing rooms and female trainers available.

Do you provide diet guidance?

Yes, diet guidance is included in our Premium and Elite membership plans. Our certified nutritionists will create a customized plan based on your goals.

Is it beginner friendly?

100%. Our trainers are always on the floor to guide you through machine setups, proper form, and basic routines to get you started safely.

Is parking available?

Yes, ample parking space is available at the City Square Mall premises for our members.
